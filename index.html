Purpose


Quartey limited is built to deliver business and technical services to small and medium businesses. With a distinct focus on delivering quality holistic solutions that are compliant with the new incoming General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).
With flexible rates and job specific fixed charges a service can be tailored for your needs. Quartey Limited can design and build the solution that you need and manage the business as usual cloud infrastructure.
